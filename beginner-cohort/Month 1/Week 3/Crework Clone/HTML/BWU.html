What is Crework?
Crework is an online community that helps you learn by building better projects.
How much time would I need to spend?
You would need to spend around 20 hours a week including the sessions, meet-ups and workshops. Well, it is going to be something technical from scratch, and you want it to be perfect right? Needs some dedication then, and we totally know that you can do it!
What is the cost for the cohort?
This one is absolutely free. All we want is your undivided attention and dedication to make your best project ever.
Is it something like a classroom course?
No, this is not going to be 6 month long classroom course. It will be a mix of self-learning with application oriented techniques/ guidance. These 6 months will focus on a variety of skills needed for you to go from zero to one in web-dev. Since you'll have accountability partners and assessments throughout, you can not slack off. A win-win situation for you! You can checkout the Curriculum above for the schedule. Go visit this link for more details.
Are there any prerequisites for applying to the batch?
Yes there a few prerequisites to get in:
  1. You should have basic knowledge related to the batch you are applying to.
  2. You should have done at least one project by yourself.
  3. You should be willing to learn about building products and not just code all day.
What is the application procedure?
You can apply whenever the application window is open. Otherwise, you can join the waitlist too. You'll need to fill a form to tell us a little about:
  1. Yourself
  2. Your technical skills/ projects/ interests
This will be followed by a 1:1 discussion with one of our team members. That's it!
You₹ll hear from us within 2 weeks of your application submission.
Who will be the mentors and how will I be helped?
You can check about the mentors above. Rest assured, we have worked hard to find the best people for you.
Your mentors will help you in many ways
  1. Solve your doubts
  2. Mentor/ guide your team to build better
  3. Teach you many more concepts relevant in the industry and are hardly known to students
  4. You can actually build you skills in any niche in Web Dev, you'll always find a mentor to guide you for whichever niche you want help in.
